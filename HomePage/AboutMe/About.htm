ABOUT ME

JON WOLF

Jon Wolf has devoted the past thirty years of his life to the art and craft of photography. Equally at home shooting a portrait, a landscape, or a documentary wedding photograph, his images are defined by his love of composition and light.

Voted as one of the top 100 photographers of all time by Professional Photographer magazine, Jon’s images have been exhibited all over the world including The Getty Image Gallery in London and the Visa Pour L’Image festival in France. His life and work have been the subject of interviews in The Washington Post, Newsweek, Sueddutsche Zeitung, Adore Noir, American Photo, British Journal of Photography, The Photographer, The Master Photographer, Professional Photographer USA, Photo Pro, Rangefinder, Digital Pro, CPN, Phase One, Alien Skin, and Nikon Europe.

Throughout his career, Jon has devoted a lot of time to teaching photography to others. He has been a Nikon Ambassador since 2008 and regularly travels throughout Europe educating and inspiring photographers from all over the world. In 2012 he spent a week in France helping Sir Don McCullin take his first steps into digital capture.

Other than being a photographer, Jon is also a passionate writer, habitual music curator, and an active gamer; all which receive noticeable attention from multiple parties.
